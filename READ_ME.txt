FindMe.py give you list of all the files in your mac system, whose size is larger or equal to the size you have specified

Keith_Numbers.py will give you the list keith numbers that are x digits long. The user can specify the digits of the keith numbers
More on Keith Numbers can be found at http://mathworld.wolfram.com/KeithNumber.html

StockTicker.py gives you the last traded date and price for user specified stock symbols
